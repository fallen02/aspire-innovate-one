Green Product Evolution

We promote eco-friendly products that contribute to Environmental Conservations.

The basic concept of SII environmental management is the “Three Green” concept: Green Process, Green Products, and Green Life. Among these, Green Products (in other words, creating eco-friendly products that can contribute to environmental conservation) are a manufacturer’s mission. We as a product distributor always support green products keeping environmental sustainability in mind.

Our green product inventory is individualized by some of the factors:

  • Sustainable materials
  • Energy efficiency
  • Reduced emissions
  • Non-toxic
  • Durability and longevity
  • Minimal packaging
